ABOUT THE EVENT

IEEE CASS Student Branch Chapter IIT Roorkee in collaboration with IEEE UP Section and Delhi CASS Section is organising a 3-day workshop to discuss the aspects of reliability and robustness of VLSI Circuits led by expert academic/industrial practitioners.

Read More
Read More

KEY HIGHLIGHTS

Talks / Tutorial by leading experts in academia and industry

Reliable and robust design methods
Strategies to reduce design margins
Beyond CMOS
Device level reliability issues

Hands-on session using EDA tools

STA and SSTA using Prime Time
Design Verification using Jasper
Monte Carlo simulations using MATLAB

Poster Presentations

Excellent opportunity for interested participants
Showcase your research in front of industry and academia
Participation Certificates
